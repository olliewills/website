About me

Who am I?

Put simply, I’m a digital media professional with a background that encompasses design, development and user experience. Like a lot of people I’ve been swept up in the fast-paced development of Information Technology. I’m a people person with a keen interest in how we live our digital and every-day lives. I have a practical nature, like to get my hands dirty and enjoy solving problems, particularly when the perfect tool for the job may not be available. In work, I advocate finding the right balance between creativity, intelligent design and process and firmly believe that these three things can exist in harmony.

What can I offer?

An informed opinion

A clear voice and level head

Working directly with clients is a challenge that requires a healthy mix of confidence, clarity, knowledge and patience. I'm friendly and open by nature and am comfortable working with brands of all sizes and people of all backgrounds. I can convey an idea in straight-forward terms so that everyone understands what I'm saying.
